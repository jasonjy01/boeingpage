  • Requesting Condition Code:
    1. Open PER form: https://wcms.web.boeing.com/sites/Auburn/MS/Pages/EE_PER.aspx
    2. Select “General Support” in “Request Type” field
    3. Fill out required fields with appropriate information
    4. For “Approver Information”, fill out with the information for the EO Finance Focal

    If historical condition codes are stored in Maximo, Everett Equipment Services can help with access: http://mxesprod.web.boeing.com/maximo
    Once your account is setup your username will be “m” followed by your BEMS ID (no spaces).
    Maintenance history can be requested from Equipment Services: esmaintenanceanalysts@exchange.boeing.com
  • Submitting Cost Estimate Request to Equipment Engineering:
    1. Open PER form: https://wcms.web.boeing.com/sites/Auburn/MS/Pages/EE_PER.aspx
    2. Select “Cost Estimate” in “Request Type” field
    3. For “Approver Information,” fill out with the information for the EO Finance Focal
