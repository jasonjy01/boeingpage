  • Requesting Condition Code:

    Historical condition codes are stored in Maximo

    1. Open Production Equipment Request form
    2. Select the pdf symbol next to "Download"
    3. Under "Request Details" "Request Type" select "General Support" from the drop down options

    4. Select "Other" (Specify Project Support) and enter "Condition Code"

    5. Fill out the required information and "Route to Approver" once complete

  • Submitting Cost Estimate Request to Equipment Engineering:

    Use the Service Request link at the top of this page to contact Facilities Asset & Management

    1. Open Production Equipment Request form
    2. Select the pdf symbol next to "Download"
    3. Under "Request Details" "Request Type" select "Cost Estimate" from the drop down options

    4. Fill out the required information and "Route to Approver" once complete

  • Submitting Capital & Tooling Request:

    Email the Capital Focal Joel Rivera to initiate Capital Request

    1. Open BCA Capital & Tooling form
    2. Expand "Templates" under "Processes and Templates"

    3. Open "Capital & Tooling Request"

  • Creating Status Slide:

    1. Open the Four Square Status Template
    2. Fill out the first slide with required milestones and project information

    3. Coordinate with the finance team for spending plans on the second page

    4. Save to the project folder on the EO server for reference

  • Expense Costs:
    An acquired asset cost is the combination of its purchase, installation, spares, and accessories.
    For assets that remain below the $5,000 threshold, we have to determine who will be maintaining the assets through an ID number.
    The acquisition cost is less than $5,000.

    Requests for Quotes must be submitted via SAP Ariba and select the following Footprints Ticket:

    * For the following Installation Request, the Equipment #, Location,, Billing Org, and G/L Account is found on Maximo by asset number
    A Planned Job Request must be filled and sent to the Maintenance Analyst (shari.k.helgeson@boeing.com).
  • Tangible Capital Assets:
    A single Boeing asset, a grouping of interdependent assets, or an original complement of low-cost equipment.
    General purpose in nature.
    The acquisition cost is equal to or greater than $5,000.
    The expected service life is equal to or greater than one year.
  • Original Complement of Low-Cost Capital Equipment:
    A grouping of assets.
    General purpose in nature.
    The acquisition cost is equal to or greater than $100,000 and can include multiple asset categories and ABU funding sources.
    The expected service life is equal to or greater than one year.
  • Internal-Use Software:
    A single Boeing asset or a grouping of interdependent assets.
    General purpose in nature.
    The acquisition cost is equal to or greater than $10 million.
    The service life is equal to or greater than one year.
